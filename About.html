About Jollibee

Welcome to Jollibee, the largest fast food chain brand in the Philippines with over 1,500 stores across 17 countries. As the dominant market leader in the Philippines, Jollibee surpasses all other multinational fast food brands combined. Our commitment to the highest standards of food quality, service, and cleanliness ensures that we serve great-tasting, high-quality, and affordable food. Indulge in our superior-tasting Chickenjoy, mouth-watering Yumburger, delicious Jolly Spaghetti, and other delectable offerings. With an aggressive international expansion, we now have over 270 branches worldwide, bringing the Jollibee experience to the United States, Canada, Hong Kong, Macau, Brunei, Vietnam, Singapore, Malaysia, Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain, Italy, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
